📋 Report details — optional, printed on the report
Consistency Report. The submitted work is read in ~150-word chunks and measured on six dimensions per chunk — register, vocabulary, sentence complexity, argument depth, error density, cohesion — with a heatmap of where the writing shifts. Text is sent for analysis and not retained afterward. Costs 1 PaperTrail Token.
Baseline Deviation Report. The submitted work is compared against a controlled sample of the same student's known writing across eleven stylistic dimensions, each rated Within Range / Notable / Anomalous — with side-by-side passages of interest and suggested discussion questions. Stylometric scores are computed in your browser and sent as anchors. Costs 1 PaperTrail Token.

Authorship Citation Report. Send the essay with its bibliography — each source is checked for existence, and what the essay says a source says is compared against what the source actually says, with quotes checked verbatim where reachable. Facts and questions, never a verdict. Keep the citations and works-cited list in — they are the point here. From 2 PaperTrail Tokens (+1 per additional 25 citations).
